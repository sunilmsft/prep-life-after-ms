Key Questions to Ask Your Financial Planner

Offer Evaluation

  • Is the severance amount reasonable for 20+ years of tenure?
  • How will severance payments be taxed? Lump sum vs. installments?
  • What's the total value of unvested RSUs I'm forfeiting?
  • Does the offer include any accelerated vesting provisions?
  • What's the COBRA vs. marketplace vs. Costco insurance comparison?

Cash Flow & Runway

  • How many months/years of runway do we have without my MS income?
  • What's our monthly burn rate and how does it compare to wife's Costco income + severance?
  • Should we build a larger cash reserve before I leave?
  • At what point would I need to generate income from consulting/business?

Mortgage Strategy

  • Should I pay off the mortgage (or a chunk of it) with savings/stock to reduce monthly obligations?
  • What's the math: mortgage interest rate vs. expected investment returns — which wins?
  • How does paying off the mortgage affect our cash runway and liquidity?
  • Tax implications — does losing the mortgage interest deduction matter at our income level?
  • If we keep the mortgage, does refinancing make sense at current rates?
  • Psychological factor — does eliminating the payment give peace of mind for the transition?

Investment & Tax Strategy

  • Should I sell MSFT shares for diversification before or after leaving?
  • Tax-loss harvesting opportunities in the departure year?
  • Roth conversion strategy — lower income year = opportunity?
  • 401(k) rollover timing — before or after leaving? Direct to Roth?
  • Mega Backdoor Roth — any final contributions to maximize?
  • Capital gains management given reduced income year

Insurance Deep Dive

  • Health insurance: compare COBRA, wife's Costco plan (add kids), ACA marketplace
  • Life insurance: convert MS group policy or get new term life? Cost comparison?
  • Do I need umbrella / personal liability insurance changes?
  • Long-term disability — do I need private coverage?

Business Planning (AI SMB Venture)

  • Best business structure (LLC, S-Corp) for consulting + AI venture?
  • Tax advantages of self-employment (Solo 401k, SEP IRA, health insurance deduction)?
  • How much startup capital should we earmark?
  • Should the business be a separate entity from consulting?

Long-term Planning

  • How does this change our retirement timeline / projections?
  • Social Security optimization — what's my projected benefit at 62/67/70?
  • Estate planning — do we need to update wills, trusts, beneficiaries?
  • Kids' education funding — 529 plans on track?

Insurance Action Items

HSA Strategy

💡 HSA is a Superpower Asset Your HSA balance is yours forever, tax-free for medical expenses at any age. After 65, it works like a traditional IRA for non-medical expenses. Keep it invested and growing. Save all medical receipts — you can reimburse yourself years later.
